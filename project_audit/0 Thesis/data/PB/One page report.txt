| Title:  Design and Implementation of Smart Electric Vehicle |
| Abstract:This project presents the design and development of an intelligent electric vehicle (EV) system that integrates GPS, GSM, and temperature sensors to enhance the safety, efficiency, and performance of the vehicle. The system uses a brushless DC (BLDC) motor, a motor controller, and a battery management system to optimize energy usage and extend the vehicle's range. The GPS module enables real-time navigation and location tracking, while the GSM module facilitates communication between the vehicle and external systems. The temperature sensors monitor the vehicle's temperature and prevent overheating. With features like predictive maintenance, emergency alerts, and remote diagnostics, the system is made to offer a smooth and intelligent driving experience. The initiative intends to contribute to the development of sustainable transportation solutions and promote the wider use of EVs. |
| Technology used: IoT, Wireless monitoring & controlling | Applications:I. To efficiently manage and optimize the vehicle’s battery usage and power consumption.II. To provide the most energy efficient route based on battery levels, traffic conditions, and weather.III. Predict and prevent potential vehicle issues before they occur, reducing downtime and repair costs. |
